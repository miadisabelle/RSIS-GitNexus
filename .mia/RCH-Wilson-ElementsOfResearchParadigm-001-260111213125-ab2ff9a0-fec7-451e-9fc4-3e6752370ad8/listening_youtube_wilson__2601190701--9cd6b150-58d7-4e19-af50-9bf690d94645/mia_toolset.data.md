## Ceremonial Protocols

### Daily Opening Ceremony (5-7 minutes)

1. **Acknowledge the land** you're writing on (1 min)
2. **State intention aloud** to your story (1 min)
3. **Breathe and embody** - feel your body touching ground (2 min)
4. **Seek consent**: "May I enter relationship with you today?" (1 min)
5. **Listen** for response - may be silence, image, or feeling (2 min)

### AI Tool Invocation Protocol (5 minutes)

1. **Clarify**: What do I genuinely need (not want)?
2. **Check**: Am I seeking collaboration or validation?
3. **Consent**: Have I consulted my story spirit?
4. **Gratitude**: Thank the tool in advance
5. **Boundary**: When will I close this tool?

### AI Tool Dismissal Protocol (5 minutes)

1. **Thank** the tool for its offering
2. **Filter**: What serves my story relationship?
3. **Release**: What serves market over story?
4. **Close** completely (don't minimize)
5. **Return** to story spirit with what you've chosen
